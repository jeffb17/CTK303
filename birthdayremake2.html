Baby Boy: By Beyoncé ft Sean Paul

My Birthday is November 11, 2003.

"Baby Boy" is a song by American singer Beyoncé, featuring Jamaican rapper Sean Paul, from her debut solo album, Dangerously in Love (2003). It was also included on the reissue of Paul's second album, Dutty Rock (2002). Both Beyoncé and Paul co-wrote the song with Robert Waller, Jay-Z, and Scott Storch, who produced it with Beyoncé.
